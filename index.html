Basic Grammar Rules

  • Start each sentence with a capital letter.
  • Use "A" before consonant sounds, "An" before vowel sounds.
  • He / She / It uses the verb with "-s".
  • Every English sentence must have a subject.
  • Adjectives do not change form.
